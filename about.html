ABOUT

My name is Felix Holzbauer and I am a 18-year-old digital Illustrator. I discovered my passion for illustration over 3 years ago and have since honed my skills to specialize in animal and character illustrations. As a creative and imaginative individual, I am able to bring ideas and concepts to life through my illustrations. I am also highly skilled in using Clip Studio and have a keen attention to detail and good hand-eye coordination. My process begins with understanding the project's requirements and brainstorming potential solutions. I then create rough sketches, which I then refine and polish to produce the final illustration. I am dedicated to delivering high-quality, visually striking works that effectively communicate the desired message
